# TrendScout User Flow

## Product Vision

TrendScout is an AI Trend Intelligence Platform for cross-border e-commerce sellers.

The platform helps sellers discover emerging trends before they become mainstream and identify product opportunities earlier than competitors.

---

## Target Users

* TikTok Shop Sellers
* Shopify Sellers
* Independent E-commerce Operators

---

## User Problem

Current workflow:

1. Browse TikTok for hours
2. Search Amazon best sellers
3. Analyze competitors manually
4. Guess which products may become popular

Pain Points:

* Time consuming
* Information scattered across platforms
* Difficult to predict trend lifecycle
* Hard to identify profitable SKUs

---

## User Goal

Help sellers answer:

"What should I sell next month?"

instead of

"What is selling today?"

---

## Core User Flow

### Step 1: Opportunity Discovery

User opens TrendScout.

System shows:

* Top Opportunities This Week
* Opportunity Score
* Trend Confidence

User identifies promising opportunities.

---

### Step 2: Trend Analysis

User clicks a trend.

System displays:

* Trend Score
* Growth Rate
* Trend Stage
* Primary Market
* AI Insight

User understands why the trend matters.

---

### Step 3: Opportunity Evaluation

System generates:

* Forecast
* Estimated Lifecycle
* Recommended SKUs
* Risk Assessment

User evaluates business potential.

---

### Step 4: Product Decision

User selects a SKU opportunity.

Expected outcome:

Become an early supplier before the trend reaches peak competition.

---

## Product Value

TrendScout transforms trend signals into actionable product opportunities.

Trend Discovery
→ Trend Analysis
→ SKU Recommendation
→ Product Decision

---

## Success Metric

Reduce product research time from several hours to a few minutes while increasing the probability of discovering emerging opportunities.
